# Solace

A mental wellness platform built for young people who need someone to talk to. Sometimes we all need a safe space, you know?

**Live Demo:** https://solace-drab.vercel.app

## What is this?

Solace is basically a mental health companion that uses AI to help you track your moods, chat when you need to vent, find calming music, and connect with others going through similar stuff.

## Features that actually work

- **AI Chatbot** - Talk to an AI that actually listens and responds with empathy (powered by Google's Gemini)
- **Mood Tracker** - Log how you're feeling throughout the day, see patterns over time
- **Music Therapy** - Get music recommendations based on your current mood via YouTube
- **Community** - Anonymous posts where you can share what's on your mind
- **Gamification** - Earn badges and points for taking care of your mental health (because why not make it fun?)
- **Breathing Exercises** - Guided meditation and yoga when you need to chill

## Tech Stack

Built with :

**Backend**
- Node.js + Express 
- MongoDB for storing everything
- Google Cloud AI (Vertex AI, Natural Language API)
- JWT authentication because we care about security!!
- Sentiment analysis to detect when someone might need extra support

**Frontend**
- Just vanilla HTML/CSS/JS 
- TailwindCSS 
- Chart.js for the mood graphs
